Summary

Austin presents a specialty coffee market with these characteristics: high household incomes ($91,501 median), educated population (53.8% bachelor's+), and validated premium pricing ($4-16 range). The market has numerous specialty roasters and shows 9.5% annual growth.

Recent Competitive Activity

  • La La Land (South Congress, July 2025): Food-first Instagram concept
  • Black Fox (Downtown, late 2025): NYC-based, in-house baking in NYC locations
  • Desnudo: Expanding locally with community programming

Key Considerations

  • Downtown: Highest incomes ($154K median), dense weekday traffic, premium rents ($40-60/sq ft)
  • South Congress: Tourist corridor, high visibility, La La Land competition, $40-50+/sq ft
  • Mueller: Family community, lower rent ($38-45/sq ft), landlord seeking coffee tenant

Premium rents ($164K-240K annual for 3,000 sq ft) require strong volume (300-500+ daily customers) to achieve sustainable economics. The market rewards quality execution, authentic community integration, and clear differentiation.
